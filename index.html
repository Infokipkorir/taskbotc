FAQ
How do I get paid?
›
Payments are sent via M-Pesa once the withdraw feature is enabled. Your earnings accumulate in your balance and you can request a payout anytime.
How long does task approval take?
›
Task submissions are reviewed within 24–48 hours. You'll receive a notification once your task is approved or rejected.
How does the referral program work?
›
Share your unique referral code. When a friend registers using your code and completes their first task, you earn $1.00 credited to your balance.
What types of tasks are available?
›
Tasks include quick actions, surveys, social media tasks, video watching, and app installs. New tasks are added regularly.
